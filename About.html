What is Meal Prepping?

Meal prepping is simply preparing your meals in advance typically with the goal to have a better diet/more balanced meals.

Benefits of Meal Prepping

Meal prepping has many benefits such as:

  • Can save money and time
  • Allows you to help control your weight due to having balanced meals with a specified amount of portions
  • Allows you to have a better balanced diet
  • As you know what you are eating on each day it reduces the stress of having to wonder what you are going to cook
